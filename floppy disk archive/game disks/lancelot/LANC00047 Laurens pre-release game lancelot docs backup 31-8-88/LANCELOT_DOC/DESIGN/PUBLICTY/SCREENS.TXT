        ---------------------------------------------------- 

Use an Atari ST with colour monitor. 

Turn it on; put the "Lancelot" playtest disc in a double-sided drive; 
double click on the appropriate drive icon and then on GAME.PRG. 

When the game is running, it will occasionally pause with <More> on 
the bottom line.. press any key to continue. 

When the game asks, "Shall we start at the beginning?", take the 
opportunity to switch to big text: PRESS F1 before entering YES. 

* Take picture 1 when the game prompts for your next command. 

* Enter EAST then CHALLENGE KNIGHT and take picture 2 when the game 
  asks if Lancelot accepted his surrender. 

* Enter YES and E.E.E and take a picture if you like. 

* Enter E.E to reach the city streets. Type FIND MERLIN (but do not 
  press Return yet) and take picture 3. 

* Press Return. When the game has printed "..fortold to me in a dream" 
  and is waiting for you to press a key, take picture 4. 

* Enter N.Z.Z and RUN TO LAWNS and EXAMINE SIEGES . When the game 
  pauses in the middle of describing the seats, take picture 5. 

* Enter N and ARTHUR, HELLO

* Enter RUN TO WESTMINSTER.S when there, type RIDE TO MELIAGAUNT'S 
  CASTLE (but do not press Return yet) and take picture 6. 

* Enter LOOK and then EXAMINE DRAWBRIDGE and take picture 7. 

* Enter RUN TO LOGRIS then GO TO TURRET.IN.CLOSE SHUTTERS and 
  KILL SOMEONE and GO TO TINTAGEL and LOOK  Then take picture 8. 

* Enter SE. WAIT FOR CART AND GET IN CART then WAIT FOR SHIPS.LOOK 
  and take picture 9. 

* Enter RESTART and answer NO to the first question. Then enter 
  PRAY.E.E.NE.NE and MAKE SIGN OF CROSS and take picture 10. 

* Enter GO TO PAVILION then KNIGHT,GAWAIN and GO TO PAVILION and 
  IN . When the game asks <More>, take picture 11. 

* Enter EAT CAKE and EAT CAKE and EAT CAKE then GO TO CLIFF and 
  GO TO NACIEN.IN IN and PRAY. When the game asks <More>, take 
  picture 12.
