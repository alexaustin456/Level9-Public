ST Time & Magik has:
 colour and b&w pictures;
 edit previous commands, by cursor keys;
 SAVE/RESTORE to disc;
 RAM SAVE/RESTORE in memory;
 UNDO (take back moves);
 BIG TEXT (press F1 and/or F2).

Continuous demonstration: WAIT FOR THE FIRST PICTURE TO FINISH LOADING
(the border and clock), then press Alternate R instead of 1, 2 or 3.

Compatibility: the adventure interpreter on this disc also works with
Knight Orc and Gnome Ranger. Copy your original and replace GAME.PRG
by the new one.

Please remember that Level 9 games are protected by copyright and take a

only as long as you have the original game.
