ST Ingrid's Back! has:
 pictures on colour and b&w displays;
 edit previous commands, by cursor
  keys and Backspace;
 SAVE/RESTORE to disc;
 RAM SAVE/RESTORE in memory;
 UNDO (retrace your steps);
 big text (press F1 and/or F2).

Continuous demonstration. WAIT UNTIL 
THE FIRST PICTURE (OUTSIDE THE GREEN 
GNOME) HAS LOADED, then press 
Alternate R instead of a gnumber.

Compatibility: GAME.PRG on this disc
should work with games from Knight Orc 
onwards. Copy your original disc and
replace its GAME.PRG with the gnew one.

Please remember that Level 9 games are
protected by copyright and take a long
time to produce, so only make one
copy, for your own use, to keep only
as long as you have the original game.
