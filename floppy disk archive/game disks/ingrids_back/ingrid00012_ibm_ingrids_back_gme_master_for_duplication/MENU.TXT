$1       1 for fast MGA/not-so-slow EGA,

$1       4 for screen-switch between text-only/CGA low-res.

$1       6 for screen-switch on EGA card EGA monitor
$1   EE. Loading screen picture number (00=no title).

$1    F. Board type
$1    G. Monitor type (0=any)
$1    H. Prefered width/characters (1=40, 0=any)
