ST Lancelot has:
 pictures on colour and b&w displays;
 edit previous commands, by cursor
  keys and Backspace;
 SAVE/RESTORE to disc;
 RAM SAVE/RESTORE in memory;
 UNDO (retrace your steps);
 big text (press F1 and/or F2).

Continuous demonstration: WAIT FOR THE
FIRST PICTURE TO FINISH LOADING, then
press Alternate R instead of answering
YES or NO.

Compatibility: GAME.PRG on this disc
works with Knight Orc, Time & Magik
and Gnome Ranger. Copy your original
disc and replace its GAME.PRG with
the new one.
